MYST MAZE

Remastered-(ish)

Legends speak of the mysterious Myst Maze and the treasure held within. Nobody has been able to navigate the traps and reach the end goal. One boy, armed with magical spells he found in the maze, plans on navigating it and reaching the treasure.
